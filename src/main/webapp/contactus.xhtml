Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a flight?

You can book a flight through our online booking system or by calling our customer service hotline.

What is the baggage allowance?

The standard baggage allowance is 23kg for checked luggage and 7kg for carry-on luggage. Additional baggage can be purchased online.

How can I change or cancel my booking?

You can change or cancel your booking through our website under the 'Manage Booking' section or by contacting our customer service.

What are the check-in options?

You can check-in online, via our mobile app, or at the airport. Online and mobile check-in open 24 hours before departure.

How can I earn frequent flyer miles?

You can earn frequent flyer miles by booking flights with us and through our partner airlines. Visit our frequent flyer page for more details.

What should I do if my flight is delayed or cancelled?

If your flight is delayed or cancelled, you will be notified via email or SMS. You can also check the flight status on our website or app.
